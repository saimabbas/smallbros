8888 Small Bros that are here to remind you that you are never alone in this journey! SmallBros believe in creating a unique environment and community, while combining the physical and digital worlds in a way that will enable all Small Bros to connect with each other, create strong friendships, and foster individual growth. We want to remind people that they are never alone in this journey.

1. Custom VR/PC Hub

The foundation of the Small Bros community.
A custom VR Hub will be available to all Small Bros holders.
Conferences, events, hangouts, games, giveaways, etc for the Bros to meet and have fun.
Phase 1 Hub will be accessible with or without VR. Hosted on AltspaceVR (Server available on a VR headset, PC or Macbook).

2. Bro's Merch

Merch Store (IRL & VR) - Creating a high-end boutique exclusively for the Bros holders.
All products will be accessible in real life via exclusive holder pass on our website & will be shown as 3D mockups in our VR World.
Only available IRL throughout phase one.

3. Bro's Charity

The team will donate 20ETH to the chosen community with the goal to support Mental Health.
Our team members will be flying out to the location to interact and support the community. The journey will be documented and shared on social media.

4. Bro's Giveaway

Sharing the Small Bros experience to as many Bros as possible is our ultimate goal.
Therefore, we are giving away 500x Oculus Quest 2 VR Headsets among the Small Bros holders.
A must have asset for Phase 2 in the making!

1. What's the utility of owning a Small Bros NFT?:

Each Small Bros NFT acts as an "Access Token"!

Owning a Small Bros gives you exclusive access to the greatest benefits developed for the bros. Whether for the Bros' World, the exclusive merch in addition to all incredible aspects of Phase 2.0.

2. Is your contract optimized to be gas efficient?

Yes as we are using the ERC-721A.

Our contract will be optimized to enable a low gas fee experience as well as being audited by a third party to ensure security and safety. Having in mind our long-term vision with the project, we want to make sure our first launch is flawless.
